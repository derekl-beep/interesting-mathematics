Probability

  • What is the expectation of the sum of two (independent) dice?

  • Suppose the probability of at least one bus arriving in 30 min is 97%. Give the expression of the probability that no bus arriving in 10 min.
    Follow-ups:

    • Discuss any assumption you have made.
    • Try to approximate the answer.
